Telangana Scholarships Hub:

1.Mahatma Jyothiba Phule BC Overseas Vidya Nidhi, Telangana

Eligibility:


-Students belonging to the backward class are willing to pursue a degree from a foreign university. .They should have scored an aggregate mark of 60% in streams like engineering, management, pure sciences, humanities, social sciences, and agriculture sciences.
-The age limit is 30 years.
-Students must be single child.
-The annual income of the family should be less than INR 1.5
Amount:Upto 100% reimbursement in tuition fees upto NR 20,000.

5.Telangana Post Matric Scholarship (PMS) for SC/ST/BC/Disabled Category:

Eligibility:

-be a domicile of Telangana state
-belong to SC/ST/BC/EBC/Disabled Welfare category
-have passed Class 10 and pursuing studies at the post-matric level
-have passed Class 10 examination with a minimum of 75% attendance at the end of each quarter
-have annual family income lower than:
-INR 2,00,000 for SC/ST category
-INR 1,50,000 for BC/EBC/Disabled Welfare category of rural areas
-INR 2,00,000 for BC/EBC/Disabled Welfare category of urban areas

Amount:The tuition fee reimbursement will depend on the course of study.
